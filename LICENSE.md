## English

All content in this repository — including the stories, names, ages, photographs, descriptive texts and personal data of the victims — is **restricted to journalistic use**.

### Permitted

- Use in news reports, articles, documentaries, books, podcasts and other journalistic productions.
- Academic and educational research resulting in journalistic, historical or public-memory publications.

### Required

**Any use must cite the source** clearly and visibly, with the following attribution:

> Source: **Memorial Inumeráveis** — https://inumeraveis.com.br

### Prohibited

- Commercial use outside the journalistic scope.
- Decontextualized reproduction of names, stories or images.
- Training of artificial intelligence models using this memorial's data.
- Mass redistribution of the dataset (`names.json` or similar) without express authorization.

### Source code

The technical code of the site (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, hosting configuration, workflows) is free to study, reference and adapt in other projects, provided it does not replicate the victims' stories, names or images.
